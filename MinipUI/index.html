Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EV?

An electric vehicle (EV) runs on electricity stored in a battery and is much more energy-efficient than ICEVs.

Why is Emission Important?

Emissions contribute to climate change, so reducing vehicle emissions is essential for a sustainable future.

How does an EV save energy?

EVs use energy more efficiently because they convert a higher percentage of electrical energy into vehicle movement compared to traditional engines.

What are the benefits of EVs?

EVs reduce air pollution, lower greenhouse gas emissions, and often have lower maintenance costs compared to traditional vehicles.

What are the challenges of EV adoption?

Challenges include limited charging infrastructure, battery range anxiety, and higher upfront costs compared to ICEVs.
